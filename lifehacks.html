Introduction

Welcome to our website about makeup life hacks! Here you will find a lot of useful tips and recommendations that will help you look beautiful and well-groomed.

Recommendations

  • Use a makeup base for a longer lasting effect
  • Choose the right skin tone to avoid blush or too pale shade
  • Do not forget about contouring and highlighter to create volume on the face
  • Use a lipstick with a glossy finish to visually enlarge your lips
  • Do not apply too much blush - it is better to add it gradually
